Introduction

About InAmigos Foundation

Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is committed to transparent, accountable, and high-quality service. We collaborate with volunteers, communities, and corporate partners to expand access to essentials and opportunity.

Our work focuses on food relief, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and skill development.

Why the projects matter

  • Immediate reliefMeeting urgent needs with food, clothing, and care.
  • Long-term growthBuilding education and livelihood pathways.
  • Inclusive supportServing people, animals, and the environment together.
  • AccountabilityWorking with measurable, transparent outcomes.
Ongoing Projects

Community Outcomes

Real change through sustained action

The projects are built to reduce immediate hardship while creating a stronger foundation for education, self-reliance, community health, and environmental responsibility.

When families receive support, children stay engaged in learning, women gain new skills, animals are protected, and communities become more resilient over time.
