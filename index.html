Introduction

About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a youth-led, Section 8 registered non-profit headquartered at Ward No. 5, Gram Post, Sipat Ujwal Nagar, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — Pin 495555.

Operating across six flagship projects, the foundation is driven almost entirely by young volunteers and interns committed to delivering transparent, measurable community impact.

Why the projects matter

  • Immediate reliefMeeting urgent needs with food, clothing, and care.
  • Long-term growthBuilding education and livelihood pathways.
  • Inclusive supportServing people, animals, and the environment together.
  • AccountabilityWorking with measurable, transparent outcomes.
Ongoing Projects

Programs designed for real-world social impact

Each initiative addresses a specific need while contributing to a wider ecosystem of care, resilience, and opportunity.

Project Seva

Food and clothing support for underserved families and community members in need.

Project Bachpanshala

Education support for children through learning access, school guidance, and digital literacy.

Project Jeev

Animal rescue, protection, and feeding efforts that promote compassionate communities.

Project Udaan

Women empowerment through skill building, awareness, and pathways to financial independence.

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation initiatives that encourage sustainability and responsible living.

Project Vikas

Employability and skill development programs that help youth prepare for future opportunities.

Mission & Values

Real change through sustained action

The projects are built to reduce immediate hardship while creating a stronger foundation for education, self-reliance, community health, and environmental responsibility. When families receive support, children stay engaged in learning, women gain new skills, animals are protected, and communities become more resilient over time.
