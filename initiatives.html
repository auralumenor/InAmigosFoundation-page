Food & Clothing

Project Seva

Food and clothing support for underserved families and individuals facing hardship across Chhattisgarh.

500,000+ Meals served
12,000+ Families supported
78% of annual target reached
Support this project

Meet Rajan. After losing his daily-wage work during a local economic downturn, Rajan struggled to feed his family of four. Project Seva volunteers began delivering weekly food parcels and connecting him to job placement resources. Within three months, Rajan found stable employment — and now volunteers at Seva distributions himself every Sunday.

Project Bachpanshala

Education access, school guidance, and digital literacy support to help children build a stronger future.

1,000+ Children reached
Active Learning centres
65% of annual target reached
Support this project

Meet Rajesh. At 10, Rajesh had never attended school — his family couldn't afford it. Project Bachpanshala enrolled him in a local learning centre where he received free materials, tutoring, and digital literacy sessions. Two years later, Rajesh passed his Class 5 exams and told his teacher he wants to become an engineer.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Animal rescue, feeding, and protection through compassionate volunteer-led care efforts across the region.

8,500+ Animals fed
600+ Rescues completed
55% of annual target reached
Support this project

Meet Tommy. Found injured on a roadside in Raipur, Tommy was brought to Project Jeev volunteers who provided emergency treatment, steady feeding, and rehabilitation over six weeks. He was successfully adopted by a local family and sparked a wider community awareness drive about stray animal welfare in the neighbourhood.

Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Women empowerment through skills training, awareness programmes, and pathways to financial independence.

900+ Women empowered
Active Workshops & drives
70% of annual target reached
Support this project

Meet Meena. A homemaker with no formal income, Meena joined Project Udaan's tailoring and entrepreneurship workshop in 2022. Within eight months she had launched her own small tailoring business from home, earning a steady income for her family. Today, Meena runs a mentoring group for 12 other women in her village going through the same programme.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation, tree plantation drives, and sustainability awareness campaigns for communities.

10,000+ Trees planted
Active Plantation drives
82% of annual target reached
Support this project

The Nandanvan Drive. In June 2023, Project Prakriti organised a single-day plantation drive in the Nandanvan area of Raipur, mobilising 300 volunteers — including 80 school students. Over 2,000 saplings were planted in one afternoon. Local forest department officials praised the initiative and partnered with InAmigos for a follow-up drive three months later.

Employability, internship, and skill development initiatives across data operations, finance, research, content writing, digital marketing, and social work.

30,000+ Interns trained

Support this project

Meet Anil. A school dropout at 16, Anil enrolled in Project Vikas's digital skills and employability programme with no prior computer experience. After completing the 3-month course he was placed as a data entry operator at a local firm. Within a year, Anil was promoted to junior analyst — and returned to InAmigos to mentor the next batch of students.
