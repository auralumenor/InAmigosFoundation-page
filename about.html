Our Journey

From founding to lasting impact

  1. 2020

    Foundation established

    Mr. Govind Shukla founded InAmigos Foundation on September 23, 2020 in Chhattisgarh, India, with a mission to deliver practical, accountable social impact.

  2. 2021

    First projects launched

    Project Seva and Project Bachpanshala began operations, delivering food relief and education support to underserved communities across the region.

  3. 2022

    National recognitions & expansion

    Registered with NITI Aayog and achieved 80G & 12A certifications. Projects Jeev, Udaan, and Prakriti launched, expanding focus areas to animal welfare, women empowerment, and the environment.

  4. 2023

    ISO certified & CSR partnerships

    Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification and CSR-1 registration, opening pathways for corporate partnerships. Project Vikas launched, addressing youth employability and skill development.

  5. 2024

    30,000+ interns & volunteers

    The internship and volunteer programme surpassed 30,000 participants, significantly expanding the foundation's operational reach and community presence.

  6. Today

    Six projects, one mission

    InAmigos Foundation now operates six active projects with measurable outcomes across food, education, animal welfare, women empowerment, environment, and youth development — all guided by a community-first mission.
