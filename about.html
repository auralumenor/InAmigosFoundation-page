Our Journey

From founding to lasting impact

  1. 2020

    Foundation established

    Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO) founded InAmigos Foundation on September 23, 2020, at Ward No. 5, Gram Post, Sipat Ujwal Nagar, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — right in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic, when the team began distributing food and essentials to those with nothing.

  2. 2021

    First projects & internship programme

    Project Seva and Project Bachpanshala launched, delivering food relief and education support. The foundation's internship programme began attracting youth volunteers from across India, quickly becoming a flagship part of its model.

  3. 2022

    National recognitions & expansion

    Registered with NITI Aayog (NGO Darpan) and achieved 80G & 12A tax certifications. Projects Jeev, Udaan, and Prakriti launched — expanding into animal welfare, women empowerment, and environmental conservation. Over 900 women reached through Udaan.

  4. 2023

    ISO certified & CSR-1 registered

    Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification and CSR-1 registration, opening corporate partnership pathways. Project Vikas launched to formalise the internship and skill development programme. Over 10,000 trees planted through Prakriti drives.

  5. 2024

    30,000+ interns & volunteers

    The internship programme surpassed 30,000 participants across fields including data operations, finance, research, content writing, digital marketing, and social work — cementing InAmigos as one of India's most active youth-led NGOs.

  6. Today

    Six projects, one community-first mission

    InAmigos Foundation operates six active projects from Bilaspur, with 12K+ Instagram followers, 500,000+ meals served, and a growing national volunteer network — all guided by a commitment to dignity, transparency, and youth-driven impact.
