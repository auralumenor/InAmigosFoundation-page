Foundation Story

Who we are and why we exist

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is based in Chhattisgarh and works through verified programs, volunteer support, and partner collaboration.

Our registrations and certifications help ensure transparency, accountability, and long-term credibility for donors and partners.

Our focus areas

  • Food reliefImmediate support for vulnerable communities.
  • EducationAccess and learning opportunities for children.
  • EmpowermentSkills and independence for women and youth.
  • WelfareAnimal care and environmental responsibility.

Our Values

How we work and what guides us

We believe in action, dignity, and opportunity. Our programs are designed to create lasting social impact by addressing immediate needs while building pathways for sustainable change.

Through collaboration with volunteers, communities, and corporate partners, we aim to expand access to essentials and opportunity across India.

How we work

  • Community-drivenPrograms designed with local input and leadership.
  • TransparentOpen reporting and accountability for donors and partners.
  • ScalablePractical solutions that can grow to meet demand.
  • CollaborativePartnerships that amplify impact and reach.
